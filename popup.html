How it works?

  • Save your credentials: If you are not seeing the direct login buttons for both CUIMS and LMS, you have not saved your credentials. Please first save the credentials.
  • Local storage: Do not worry, these are saved on your local system and there is no way anyone can know your credentials, not even us.
  • Enable Direct Login: Check the "Enable Direct Login" checkbox to allow automatic login when you type the URL manually or click on the direct login buttons.
  • Direct login buttons: After saving the credentials and enabling direct login, you will see direct login buttons for CUIMS and LMS.
  • Automatic login: Click on where you want to login and it will log you in automatically.
  • CAPTCHA handling: If there is any CAPTCHA, it will automatically fill the CAPTCHA. If it gets it wrong, it will automatically try again and get you logged in.

Happy auto login!
